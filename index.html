Creatve Statement

My work as an artist revolves around visual mediums, focusing on photography and filmmaking. In using these visual mediums I will focus on themes of femininity, girlhood, freedom and being in your adolescence. girlhood, freedom and being in your adolescence.Through experimenting with light, textures and blending images, I will communicate these themes and share them digitally. Most of my work is inspired by my own personal experiences and the way I observe and see the world. I am interested in how girlhood and femininity are constructed and performed within digital spaces, where images are constantly circulated, fragmented, and reinterpreted. By sharing my own perspective, I aim to reclaim girlhood as something self-defined rather than idealized and consumed.
